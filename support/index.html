Gemoria Support

Support and contact information for Gemoria.

Gemoria is a mobile match-3 puzzle adventure game. This page explains how to contact us for support, privacy questions, and app issues.

Contact

For support, privacy questions, bug reports, or other app-related questions, contact:

gemoria.game@gmail.com

What to include in a support request

To help us understand the issue, please include as much of the following information as you can:

  • the device model you are using
  • the Android version, if known
  • the Gemoria app version, if known
  • which level or screen the issue happened on
  • what you expected to happen
  • what actually happened
  • whether the problem happens repeatedly or only once

Please do not send passwords, payment details, personal documents, or other sensitive information. Gemoria does not require accounts, passwords, or payment details.

Privacy questions

For information about how Gemoria uses analytics and crash reporting, please read the Privacy Policy.

You can also contact us using the support email above for privacy-related questions.

Advertising and purchases

Gemoria currently does not include advertising, in-app purchases, subscriptions, real-money coin packs, or billing features.
